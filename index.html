CANDICOINS: Sweet Feautures

Candicoins has features that can match up to modern cryptocurrencies and perform better with new technologies integrated.

  • Decentralized & No Central Authority

    In traditional fiat currencies, central authorities and banks, control the financial system. However, with cryptocurrencies, these transactions can be processed and validated by a distributed and open network, that is owned by no-one.

  • Anonymous / Pseudo-anonymous

    Since there is no need for a central authority, users do not need to identify themselves when transacting th cryptocurrency. When a transaction request is submitted, the decentralized network will check the transaction and verify it and record it on the blockchain accordingly.

  • Irreversible & Immutable (cannot be undone)

    Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible and immutable. The irreversible and immutable features of cryptocurrency means that it is impossible for anyone but the owner of the respective private key to move their digital assets and that transactions cannot be changed once it is recorded on the blockchain.

  • Limited Supply & Scarcity

    Fiat currencies (e.g. dollars, euros) have an unlimited supply, as the central banks can issue as much fiat currencies as they want. Central banks often manipulate the value of the countries’ currencies as part of its economic policies. Most countries often manipulate their currency to be inflationary over a period of time. The inflationary nature of fiat currencies would mean a decrease in the value of the currency over time.
